<h2>Japanese Learning Sentences</h2>

<h3>N5 Level</h3>
<p><strong style="color:blue;">Original:</strong> 粉雪が空から優しく降りてくる<br>
*Translation*: The powdery snow gently falls from the sky.</p>
<p><strong style="color:blue;">New Sentence (Use of "空から" - "from the sky"): </strong>太陽が空から輝いている。<br>
*Translation*: The sun is shining down from the sky.</p>

<p><strong style="color:blue;">Original:</strong> あの日見た雪の白さ<br>
*Translation*: The whiteness of the snow I saw that day.</p>
<p><strong style="color:blue;">New Sentence (Use of "～の白さ" - "the whiteness of something"): </strong>桜の花の白さが美しいです。<br>
*Translation*: The whiteness of the cherry blossoms is beautiful.</p>

<p><strong style="color:blue;">Original:</strong> 今でも夢を見ている<br>
*Translation*: Even now, I still dream.</p>
<p><strong style="color:blue;">New Sentence (Use of "夢を見ている" - "to be dreaming"): </strong>毎晩楽しい夢を見ています。<br>
*Translation*: I have pleasant dreams every night.</p>

<h3>N4 Level</h3>
<p><strong style="color:blue;">Original:</strong> 手のひらで受け止めた雪が切ない<br>
*Translation*: The snow I caught in my hand feels bittersweet.</p>
<p><strong style="color:blue;">New Sentence (Use of "切ない" - "bittersweet, poignant"): </strong>その歌を聴くと切ない気持ちになる。<br>
*Translation*: Listening to that song makes me feel bittersweet.</p>

<h3>N3 Level</h3>
<p><strong style="color:blue;">Original:</strong> あなたは立ち止まり<br>
*Translation*: You stop in your tracks.</p>
<p><strong style="color:blue;">New Sentence (Use of "立ち止まる" - "to stop in one's tracks"): </strong>考え事をして、突然立ち止まりました。<br>
*Translation*: Lost in thought, I suddenly stopped in my tracks.</p>

<p><strong style="color:blue;">Original:</strong> 恋しく思えた<br>
*Translation*: Felt so dear, missed.</p>
<p><strong style="color:blue;">New Sentence (Use of "恋しい" - "dear, missed"): </strong>故郷が恋しい。<br>
*Translation*: I miss my hometown.</p>

<h3>N2 Level</h3>
<p><strong style="color:blue;">Original:</strong> 暖かいあなたの優しさに似ている<br>
*Translation*: It’s like your warm kindness.</p>
<p><strong style="color:blue;">New Sentence (Use of "～に似ている" - "to resemble, to be like"): </strong>彼の声は父の声に似ている。<br>
*Translation*: His voice is like my father's.</p>

<p><strong style="color:blue;">Original:</strong> いとおしく見えた<br>
*Translation*: You looked so dear to me.</p>
<p><strong style="color:blue;">New Sentence (Use of "いとおしい" - "dear, precious"): </strong>子犬がとてもいとおしく感じられる。<br>
*Translation*: The puppy feels so precious to me.</p>

<h3>N1 Level</h3>
<p><strong style="color:blue;">Original:</strong> あの時触れた指先の冷たさも忘れない<br>
*Translation*: I won’t forget the coldness of your fingertips that I felt that time.</p>
<p><strong style="color:blue;">New Sentence (Use of "冷たさ" - "coldness"): </strong>冬の風の冷たさが身にしみる。<br>
*Translation*: The coldness of the winter wind chills me to the bone.</p>
