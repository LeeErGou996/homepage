<h2>Japanese Learning Summary</h2>

<h3>N5 Level</h3>
<p><strong>Original:</strong> <span style="color:blue;">粉雪</span>が<span style="color:blue;">空</span>から<span style="color:blue;">優</span>しく降りてくる<br>
*Translation*: The powdery snow gently falls from the sky.</p>
<p><strong>New Sentence:</strong> <span style="color:blue;">太陽</span>が<span style="color:blue;">空</span>から輝いている。<br>
*Translation*: The sun is shining down from the sky.</p>

<p><strong>Original:</strong> <span style="color:blue;">あなた</span>は立ち止まり<br>
*Translation*: You stop in your tracks.</p>
<p><strong>New Sentence:</strong> 私は信号で立ち止まりました。<br>
*Translation*: I stopped at the traffic light.</p>

<h3>N4 Level</h3>
<p><strong>Original:</strong> <span style="color:blue;">思い出して</span>いますか <span style="color:blue;">空</span>を見上げながら<br>
*Translation*: Do you remember as you look up at the sky?</p>
<p><strong>New Sentence:</strong> 音楽を聴きながら本を読んでいます。<br>
*Translation*: I’m reading a book while listening to music.</p>

<p><strong>Original:</strong> <span style="color:blue;">雪</span>が<span style="color:blue;">切ない</span><br>
*Translation*: The snow feels bittersweet.</p>
<p><strong>New Sentence:</strong> 映画の結末が切なくて涙が出た。<br>
*Translation*: The ending of the movie was bittersweet and made me cry.</p>

<h3>N3 Level</h3>
<p><strong>Original:</strong> いとおしく<span style="color:blue;">見えた</span><br>
*Translation*: You looked so dear to me.</p>
<p><strong>New Sentence:</strong> 赤ちゃんがとてもいとおしく感じられた。<br>
*Translation*: The baby felt so precious to me.</p>

<p><strong>Original:</strong> <span style="color:blue;">あなた</span>が楽しそうに話をしてくれた<br>
*Translation*: You happily talked with me.</p>
<p><strong>New Sentence:</strong> 友達が楽しそうに新しい仕事について話してくれた。<br>
*Translation*: My friend happily talked about their new job.</p>

<h3>N2 Level</h3>
<p><strong>Original:</strong> <span style="color:blue;">初めて</span>触れた唇の<span style="color:blue;">温もり</span>も忘れない<br>
*Translation*: I won’t forget the warmth of the first touch of your lips.</p>
<p><strong>New Sentence:</strong> 初めて会った日のことを今も覚えています。<br>
*Translation*: I still remember the day we first met.</p>

<p><strong>Original:</strong> あの日見た<span style="color:blue;">白い世界</span><br>
*Translation*: The white world I saw that day.</p>
<p><strong>New Sentence:</strong> 旅行中に見た美しい景色が忘れられません。<br>
*Translation*: I can’t forget the beautiful scenery I saw during the trip.</p>

<h3>N1 Level</h3>
<p><strong>Original:</strong> あの時触れた<span style="color:blue;">指先</span>の<span style="color:blue;">冷たさ</span>も忘れない<br>
*Translation*: I won’t forget the coldness of your fingertips that I felt that time.</p>
<p><strong>New Sentence:</strong> 過去の辛い経験を忘れないことで強くなれる。<br>
*Translation*: Remembering past painful experiences can make you stronger.</p>

<p><strong>Original:</strong> <span style="color:blue;">I still love you</span><br>
*Translation*: I still love you.</p>
<p><strong>New Sentence:</strong> <span style="color:blue;">I still remember you.</span><br>
*Translation*: I still remember you.</p>
