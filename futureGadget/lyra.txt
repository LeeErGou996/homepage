<h2>Japanese Learning Sentences</h2>

<h3>N5 Level</h3>
<p><strong style="color:blue;">Original:</strong> いつだって誰かを想う<br>
*Translation*: I always think of someone.</p>
<p><strong style="color:blue;">New Sentence (Use of "想う" - "to think of someone/something deeply"): </strong>私は君のことを毎日想っています。<br>
*Translation*: I think of you every day.</p>

<p><strong style="color:blue;">Original:</strong> ずっと一緒に居たいよ<br>
*Translation*: I want to stay together forever.</p>
<p><strong style="color:blue;">New Sentence (Use of "一緒に居たい" - "to want to stay together"): </strong>友達と一緒に居たい気持ちが強いです。<br>
*Translation*: I strongly want to stay together with my friends.</p>

<h3>N4 Level</h3>
<p><strong style="color:blue;">Original:</strong> 君の言葉泣いて笑った<br>
*Translation*: I cried and laughed at your words.</p>
<p><strong style="color:blue;">New Sentence (Use of "泣いて笑った" - "to cry and laugh"): </strong>映画を見て泣いて笑いました。<br>
*Translation*: I cried and laughed watching the movie.</p>

<p><strong style="color:blue;">Original:</strong> ほんとうの気持ち<br>
*Translation*: True feelings.</p>
<p><strong style="color:blue;">New Sentence (Use of "気持ち" - "feeling/emotion"): </strong>彼に伝えたい気持ちがあります。<br>
*Translation*: I have feelings I want to convey to him.</p>

<h3>N3 Level</h3>
<p><strong style="color:blue;">Original:</strong> 想いも溫もりの日もよみがえるね<br>
*Translation*: The memories and warmth come back.</p>
<p><strong style="color:blue;">New Sentence (Use of "よみがえる" - "to revive/come back"): </strong>この曲を聴くと、学生時代の思い出がよみがえります。<br>
*Translation*: Listening to this song brings back memories of my student days.</p>

<p><strong style="color:blue;">Original:</strong> 織姫様にはなれない<br>
*Translation*: I can't become a star like Orihime.</p>
<p><strong style="color:blue;">New Sentence (Use of "～にはなれない" - "can't become"): </strong>私はまだ先生にはなれない。<br>
*Translation*: I can't become a teacher yet.</p>

<h3>N2 Level</h3>
<p><strong style="color:blue;">Original:</strong> それも愛なのかな?<br>
*Translation*: Could that be love, too?</p>
<p><strong style="color:blue;">New Sentence (Use of "なのかな" - "I wonder if..."): </strong>これは本当に良い選択なのかな？<br>
*Translation*: I wonder if this is truly the right choice?</p>

<p><strong style="color:blue;">Original:</strong> デジャブみたいに心の奧で揺れてる<br>
*Translation*: It wavers in the depths of my heart, like déjà vu.</p>
<p><strong style="color:blue;">New Sentence (Use of "心の奥で" - "in the depths of my heart"): </strong>彼の言葉が心の奥で響きました。<br>
*Translation*: His words resonated in the depths of my heart.</p>

<h3>N1 Level</h3>
<p><strong style="color:blue;">Original:</strong> 全ての場面が戀しすぎて<br>
*Translation*: I miss every scene so much.</p>
<p><strong style="color:blue;">New Sentence (Use of "戀しすぎて" - "to miss too much"): </strong>故郷が恋しすぎて涙が出た。<br>
*Translation*: I missed my hometown so much that I cried.</p>

<p><strong style="color:blue;">Original:</strong> 新しい未来の物語の主人公へ<br>
*Translation*: To the protagonist of a new story of the future.</p>
<p><strong style="color:blue;">New Sentence (Use of "物語の主人公" - "protagonist of a story"): </strong>彼はこの物語の主人公です。<br>
*Translation*: He is the protagonist of this story.</p>
